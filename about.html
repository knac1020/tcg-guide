About Tokyo Card Guide

Tokyo Card Guide (TCG) is an English-language directory and guide for overseas collectors and tourists looking for trading card shops in Tokyo.

What we cover

🃏 We cover Pokémon, One Piece, MTG, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Dragon Ball and more — with shop-by-shop information on tax-free shopping, English-speaking staff, credit card acceptance, buyback services, oripa, and duel spaces.

Who is this for?

✈️ This site is designed for international visitors to Tokyo who want to find TCG shops, understand how to buy cards in Japan, and make the most of their visit — without needing to read Japanese.

Accuracy & updates

ℹ️ We do our best to keep shop information accurate and up to date. However, opening hours, stock, and services can change. We recommend confirming directly with the shop before visiting.

Contact

✉️ For questions, corrections, or shop listing requests, please visit our Contact page.
