Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

Tokyo Card Guide does not collect personal information directly. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about site usage, such as pages visited and device types. This data does not identify individual users.

2. Cookies

This site uses cookies through Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These cookies help us understand how visitors use the site and allow us to display relevant advertisements. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your previous visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Third-Party Links

This site contains links to external websites (such as shop websites and Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

5. Contact Form

If you use our contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent to us via Formspree. This information is used only to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
