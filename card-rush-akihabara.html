About this shop

ℹ️ One of Akihabara's largest TCG shops, known for a huge selection of Pokémon and One Piece singles, plus a dedicated MTG and Yu-Gi-Oh! corner. Popular with overseas collectors looking for graded cards.

Location & Access

🏢 Radio Kaikan, 2F — 1-15-16 Sotokanda, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo
🚶 3 min walk from Akihabara Station (Electric Town Exit)

Foreign Collector Tips

  • Tax-free available on purchases over ¥5,000
  • Staff can help locate specific cards if you show a photo
  • Get there early on weekends — the singles boxes get crowded fast
